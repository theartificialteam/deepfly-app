# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 2026**

## Introduction

DeepFly ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

## Key Points

- **All processing happens on your device** - Your photos and videos are never uploaded to our servers
- **No data collection** - We do not collect, store, or share your personal media
- **Complete privacy** - Your analysis results stay on your device

## Information We Process

### On-Device Processing
- Photos and videos you select for analysis are processed entirely on your device
- Analysis results are stored locally on your device
- No media files leave your device

### Account Information (Optional)
If you create an account:
- Email address (for account identification)
- Account preferences
- Analysis history (stored locally on device)

### Usage Data
- Number of analyses performed (stored locally)
- App usage statistics (anonymous, aggregate only)

## Data Storage

- All user data is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage
- Account data (if created) is stored securely using industry-standard encryption
- Analysis history is kept only on your device

## Data Sharing

**We do not share your data with anyone.**

- No third-party analytics
- No advertising networks
- No data brokers
- No government requests (we have no data to provide)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Delete your account and all associated data at any time
- Clear your analysis history
- Export your data (through the app settings)
- Use the app without creating an account (guest mode)

## Children's Privacy

DeepFly is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
- Email: privacy@deepfly.app

## GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Union:
- We process data based on your consent
- You have the right to access, rectify, or delete your data
- You may withdraw consent at any time
- No data is transferred outside your device

## CCPA Compliance

For California residents:
- We do not sell personal information
- We do not collect personal information for commercial purposes
- You have the right to know what data we collect (none beyond what's stated)
- You have the right to delete your data
