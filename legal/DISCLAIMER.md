## Purpose of DeepFly

DeepFly is an AI-powered application designed to analyze photos and videos for potential signs of deepfake manipulation or AI generation. The app is intended for:

- Educational purposes
- Personal awareness
- General information

## Accuracy Limitations

**DeepFly's analysis results are NOT 100% accurate.**

Our AI detection system:

- Uses heuristic-based analysis methods
- May produce FALSE POSITIVES (authentic content flagged as fake)
- May produce FALSE NEGATIVES (fake content flagged as authentic)
- Has limitations in detecting sophisticated deepfakes
- May be affected by image quality, lighting, and compression
